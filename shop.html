Directions from the nearest Station

From Mitsukoshi-mae :
On the second floor of the Kobayashi Building

  • Tokyo Metro Mitsukoshi-mae. Exit A1・A4
  • The area above the Tokyo Metro station
  • Turn at the corner near the shop with this sign: 大和屋
  • Walk along the road where you find the Smile Hotel ( スマイルホテル )
  • Along the way, you will see a sign for the Kobayashi Building; it's name looks like this in Japanese: 小林寶林堂
    Turn the corner here.
  • You will enter a narrow street; the Kobayashi Building is on your right.
  • This is what the building name looks like on the sign outside the building.
  • The first floor is an antique wares shop; the kimono shop is on the second floor.
